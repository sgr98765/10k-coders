Rohit Gurunath Sharma (born 30 April 1987) is an Indian international cricketer and the current captain of India national cricket team in all formats. Widely regarded as one of the greatest white ball batsmen of this era, Sharma is known for his timing, elegance, six-hitting abilities and leadership skills
